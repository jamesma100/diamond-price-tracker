How we estimate When you enter your data, it is entered as input into a machine learning algorithm, which outputs the predicted cost of your diamond. Our model is trained on existing data on diamond prices and their relationship to key attributes in the market. The price of a diamond is heavily dependent on its carat, cut, color, and clarity, but we also used other attributes in training our model. If you are considering purchasing a diamond, make sure it is certified.
